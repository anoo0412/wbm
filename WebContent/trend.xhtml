Online Reputation Miner

Are you an employer?

Then this is the right product for you. We understand how tedious it is for your HR team to scan through all the resumes received for a job opening and verify their integrity. A potential employee has a lot of web presence, and checking out his credentials mentioned in his/her resume is of utmost importance to your candidate selection procedure. This is where our product, code named MIS510Prj, comes to the rescue.

Are you a job seeker?

Then you will definitely land your dream job with the help of MIS510Prj. How many times have you thought that you deserved a particular job and you almost had it, when rejection struck? Don’t worry; only reason for such a thing to have happened is distracting information about you lying around on the web. You need to ensure your online presence is clean and supportive about your professional image.
